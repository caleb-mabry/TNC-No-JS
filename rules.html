No Spamming

This includes, but is not limited to, constantly changing music in a short span of time, sending overly large messages with no reason, sending multiple messages in a row with no reason, and excessive use of caps.

No Music Cutting

Do not change a song that has just been played by another user. Please change a song when either, the song is looped automatically by the server, the song is over, or the user who played the song gave you permission to cut it.

Be Respectful

Everyone is apart of the server to enjoy themselves, and others. Respect everyone. We understand not everyone can be friends, and even friends act in aggression as a joke, but be weary of the people you are around.

  • This includes the server itself, please try to be respectful of it, and do not try to do actions that may harm it in some way. This includes, but is not limited to, locking areas under multi clients, advertising and hogging characters.

Language

We are completely open to any and all languages, but please respect that this is a primarily English server. As such, please maintain English when others around cannot understand you.

No Discrimination

We try to ensure everyone is treated equally, so do not discriminate anyone for race, belief, or religion.

Under the same rule, do not force your beliefs upon others. This is just as bad as discrimination.

Trials

If a trial is in session, do not interrupt if you are not apart of the case. If you wish to communicate with the case players, the OOC chat is available to you.

  • No cases or roleplay in lobby (Area 0)
  • If a character is needed for a case, then the participants get first right at them. If you are on a character that is needed, please switch to another.
  • If a case involved excessive gore, sexual content or touchy topics, you must lock the room.

Loophole

If you break a rule, and try to escape it's intended purpose by either picking at faulty Wording, or trying to dodge with specifics, your punishment may become more severe.

  • Everyone is entitled to plead your case, if a rule is broken. But do not debate your punishment, that will be decided by staff. Fighting against a punishment may lead to a harsher one.

Gamemodes

Gamemodes (anything that is not cases) can take up a maximum of 6 areas at once. If you have a concern about a gamemode, contact a mod.

  • Pushing a GM to end their gamemode can, and will fall under harassment. Contact a mod if you are getting harrassed.
  • On the other hand, if a gamemode has taken many hours, or has intentionally occupied areas in a way that’s against the player base, contact a mod for your concerns

Explicit Content

Explicit conversation, content, links, and roleplay is prohibited in the server lobby. This includes specific topics delving into and links to gore, pornagraphy, and scandalous roleplay. This does NOT forbid the acknowledgement of sex/gore/ERP/etc existing, but rather specific, explicit details under these umbrellas. If a topic steers in this direction, the onus is on the user to divert to direct messaging, taking it further until reminded by a moderator past a single warning will not be tolerated. Other, specific cases may apply at a moderator's discretion.

As a rule of thumb for where "the line" is with potentially sensitive subjects, consider where the Ace Attorney franchise lands on an ESRB/PEGI rating. New and old users alike can expect themes of murder/violence present in an Ace Attorney themed setting, but these games avoid most explicit material. The exception here is simple explatives, which may be avoided in Ace Attorney, but will not be censored in this server outside of derrogatory language and other slurs.

Please note these rules differ for cases and other roleplays/chats hosted in non-lobby areas. Other such events and especially cases, being that they portray criminal trials, are likely to feature explicit content like gore, or in some cases, sexual content in some capacity, but there is a line that will be drawn at a mods discretion. If you even think you are approaching a line with your case's content, you should have a moderator look it over. In most cases, the worst result of this would be a request to lock the area when you run the case.

Custom Content

Custom content is strictly prohibited in lobby. In cases, you may use custom content, but ONLY if you lock the area.

  • As a note, please do not mess with TNCs files. I know it’s tempting to use custom emotes for characters, but it makes things harder for the members of TNC. So if you use custom content, please use characters that are NOT present on the server character list.
  • In the RP areas, or during an RP/GoM, you are allowed custom content as well. Same as point one, please only add custom content not on the character select, and only if the GM is accepting of custom content.
  • If an RP/GoM is in progress, please respect the area(s) being used. Whoever was there first, has priority over the areas. Please work this out in a civilized manner, by asking politely (The global chat can be used with /g).
